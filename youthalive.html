About Omolara

Omolara is a skilled tax professional with over ten (10) years’ experience in employment tax compliance, global mobility, compensation structuring, and customer relations management. She has provided strategic guidance and compliance management support to both domestic and multinational corporations.

A devoted child of God, Omolara has a track record of excellent client service delivery and coaching. In line with her passion to share knowledge and help others grow, Omolara volunteers for outreaches and support.

About Mayowa Oduwobi

Mayowa Oduwobi is a guidance counselor, educational consultant, and life skills coach specializing in measurement and evaluation. Currently pursuing a PhD in Measurement and Evaluation, Mayowa is passionate about empowering others to reach their full potential, grounded in a strong foundation of faith which deeply influences her commitment to uplifting others in their educational and personal journeys.

About David Olawade

David Olawade ("Masterpiece") is a multifaceted minister, author, researcher, and scholar. With 15 published books and 70+ research papers, Masterpiece inspires globally. He leads Excellent Tribe Global, equipping ministers and leaders. Holding postgraduate certifications in theology and public health, fellowships with the Royal Society of Public Health, and 50+ professional certifications, Masterpiece works as a Senior Research and Innovation Project Facilitator with the NHS, England and as a lecturer at UK universities. Recognized by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation and nominated for Forbes 30 Under 30 Africa, Masterpiece consults for WHO, UNICEF, and more. Married with two children, he resides in the UK, empowering the next generation.
